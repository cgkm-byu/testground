AnyWeather target audience

Audience description

The target audience is composed of adults between 18-50 years who need to know the weather forecast in order to perform their daily activites (work, school,...) or seasonal plans such as vacations.

Personas

Daily worker

  • Fictional Name: John Smith
  • Job title: Store Manager
  • Demographics:
    • 36 years old
    • Married
    • Father of two children
    • Fishing enthusiast
    • Bachelor's degree on administration
  • Goals and tasks: He seeks to improve his store sales by making efective predictions. His tasks include:
    • Setting up the rooster for the work week.
    • Preparing a budget to cover expenses.
    • Evaluating employees perfomances.
  • Environment: Spends his day between the store floor and his office, checking on his employees and the store documentation. Checks e-mails as soon as receives notifications on his phone. Uses news websites to know what is happening around the world.

Off track student

  • Fictional Name: Elizabeth Jones
  • Job title: Student
  • Demographics:
    • 22 years old
    • Single
    • Adventurer
    • Undergraduate marketing media major
  • Goals and tasks: She is looking for new experiences while on her off semester. Her tasks include:
    • Planning meaningful adventures for herself and her friends.
    • Attending parties and social events to network with potential furute clients.
  • Environment: Spends most of her day on social media, looking for cool places to visit and events to attend.

Scenarios

John

John visits AnyWeather daily to check if the day will be busy or not since sales are normally better on sunny days.

John likes to see the forecast for the week so he knows to schedule his best salesman to the sunny days and prepare for any snow days.

The weekend is near and John has been waiting to go on a fishing trip, he visits AnyWeather to ensure that he won't get as wet as the fishes.

Elizabeth

Elizabeth visits AnyWeather as part of a plan of hiking trail with her friends. She must know that there won't be rain or snowto make sure that they will have a nice hike with low chance of accidents.

Elizabeth was invited to a social event and wants to make sure that she won't need to carry an umbrela and wear boots, so she visits AnyWeather to look at tomorrow's forecast.
