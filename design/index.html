  • Hick's Law

  • Amazon
  • Website:amazon.com
  • This page exemplifies Hick's law in many ways but the one depicted on the screenshot is the shopping menu, which instead of giving the visitor the infinity of products amazon has to offer it summarize those by giving the user category options making it easier for the potential customer to find what they are looking for without getting lost.

  • Fitt's Law

  • Ebay
  • Website:ebay.com
  • This page exemplifies Fitt's law by the sizing of buttons and objects and their positioning on the page creating an ideal distance for mobile users that can confortably use the website.
